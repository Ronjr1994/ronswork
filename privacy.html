Privacy · Last updated July 16, 2026

Clear information. No hidden submission.

Ron's Work is a static portfolio hosted on GitHub Pages. The guided assistant runs in your browser. Project information is not automatically sent unless a secure intake endpoint is configured in a future release and you deliberately press the send button.

Do not submit patient names, medical records, passwords, account credentials, protected health information, or confidential files through the public portfolio.

Information you choose to provide

The project brief may ask for your name, email address, company, current challenge, desired result, tools, volume, timeline and engagement type. In the current public build, choosing “Compose email” opens your email application with a prepared draft. You decide whether to send it.

Local draft saving

Draft saving is optional and off by default. When enabled, only non-contact project details are saved in your browser's local storage. Names, email addresses and the safety confirmation are not stored. Use the “Clear saved draft” control to remove the local copy.

Guided assistant

The assistant uses browser-based matching rules to suggest services and next steps. It does not send your messages to an external AI provider in the current release and should not be treated as professional, legal, medical or compliance advice.

External links

Links to LinkedIn, Upwork, GitHub Pages projects and email services are governed by the privacy practices of those providers. Ron's Work does not control their data handling.
